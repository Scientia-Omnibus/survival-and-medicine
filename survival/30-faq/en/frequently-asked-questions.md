## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can this app work with ultra power saving mode on Samsung devices?**

A: It does not seem to be possible — apps must be whitelisted by Samsung and there is no option to do this from the app side. If you know a way, contributions are welcome.

**Q: I found a typo. Can I fix it?**

A: Yes — contributions to fix typos or improve content are welcome.

**Q: Can we have translations?**

A: Yes, but it is complicated. Partial translation support will be added once the current content is further improved. Translators are welcome to help convert image-based lists into markdown, which is easier to translate.

**Q: The app says offline — why does it still need internet permission?**

A: The app works without internet in survival situations but may use the internet if available. You can put your device in airplane mode to prevent network access.

**Q: Can you please not express political opinions in this app?**

A: No. Different opinions are part of open discourse.
